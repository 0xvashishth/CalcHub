## CALCULATOR FUNCTIONS

The calculator is designed to find the modulus and inverse modulus of two entered numbers.

For example:

5 MOD 3 = 2

5 MOD 20 = 5

5^(-1) MOD 11 = 9

5^(-1) MOD 15 = INVERSE DOES NOT EXIST

The calculator is made using HMTL5, CSS3 & JS.
